About ORCA exchange and App

Orca is a permissionless exchange based on Solana that operates in the pool of automated liquid market makers (CLAMM). On the Orca app, users can trade liquid SPL tokens and invest in new SPL token pairs. Experience

Founded by Yutaro Mori and Ori Kwan, Orca first launched in February 2021 with the goal of creating a Solana-based solution that would "provide market value," limit volatility, and improve user experience, such as displaying icons in other apps.
According to a February 2021 announcement, Orca will be the third application to launch on Solana. On August 9, 2021, the project team launched ORCA, an order-book exchange (DEX) for Solana via Serum. August. On December 12, 2021, mining fees paid in ORCA were introduced to encourage the distribution of money to certain pools.
As of November 2021, the project team has raised more than $1.2 million in mint rewards through the Orcanauts NFT collection. The money raised will be donated to charities through the Orca Climate Fund, a private business that donates to organizations dedicated to protecting the environment. In April 2022, the project team launched Orca DAO, a system of governance for ORCA token holders. That same month, the group launched Whirlpools, an Orca pool where users can choose a price range to provide money. Orca previously released Aquafarms, its standard pool that allows users to deposit all their money. In July 2022, Orca DAO approved the creation of the Orca Steering Committee, a seven-member body that will jointly manage the protocol and ORCA token holders. In November 2023, the project team launched the "Liquidity Terminal", a new interface for interacting with Orca, which includes indicator information such as candlestick charts, previous price, depth of water, and price and market capitalization.

Supremacy of ORCA in Solana Universe

Orca is a Solana-based AMM DEX that creates user-experiences in the financial (DeFi) space. Orca eases the complexity and positions itself as the premier platform for cryptocurrency trading in the Solana area. In addition to its ease of use, it also presents ways to earn money by sending tokens and transaction fees through new pools. Orca was widely praised for its easy-to-use features such as the Direct Price Indicator, Magic Bar, and Your Indicator Panel, which cemented its position as a leading DEX and reshaped Solana's blockchain landscape.
